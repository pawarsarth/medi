1.Introduction:
The login features allows user to use the features of the website.This section provides the instructions to login and manage your accout.

2.Logging In:
To login to website follow these steps:
- Step 1: Go to the website.
- Step 2: On to the login screen, enter your username and password.
- Step 3: Click on the login button.
- Step 4: If the login is valid then,you will be looged in and redirected to dashboard.

3.Forgot Password:
If you forgot your password, folow those steps to reset if:
- Step 1: On the login screen, click on the "Forgot Password" link.
- Step 2: First choose the correct  security keys and enter new username and new password.
- Step 3: Once the password is reset then, you can login using the new password.

4.Troubleshooting:
If you are facing some issues while logging to website,try the following steps:
- Step 1: Verify that you are using correct username and password.
- Step 2: Check your internet connection to ensure it is stable.
- Step 3:Check your browser cache and try to logging in again.
